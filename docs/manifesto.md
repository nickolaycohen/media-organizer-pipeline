# Personal Development Manifesto

## 1. Guiding Intent

A commitment to organizing, preserving, and improving the quality of life through intentional tools and thoughtful reflection. This document is a living reflection of my priorities and evolution.

---

## 2. Current Domains of Focus

### 📸 Photo Memory Curation

**Why**: Preserve family memories for future generations in a meaningful, shareable format.
**Current system**: Apple Photos + Google AI + curated export pipeline
**Key values**: Human review > algorithm, preservation > automation, visibility > storage

### 📬 Digital Hygiene: Inbox Maintenance

**Why**: Reduce mental clutter, reclaim time, and make space for what matters.
**Goals**:

- Achieve a maintainable inbox flow (e.g., Inbox Zero or Weekly Cleanup)
- Identify and silence low-value sources
- Tag/archive important threads
  **Support tools**: Filters, summaries, AI-prioritized triage

### 💬 Communication & Expression

**Why**: To improve connection, clarity, and personal presence.
**Goals**:

- Practice concise, thoughtful written communication
- Reflect and revise when possible (journaling, letter writing)
- Use AI to simulate conversation or generate drafts

## 3. AI as a Partner

- Aids decision-making without replacing reflection
- Tracks and re-presents context when needed
